# Lung Cancer Risk & Survival Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes lung cancer patient data to identify patterns in demographics, risk factors, treatment effectiveness, and survival outcomes.
An interactive dashboard was developed using **Power BI** to visualize key insights and support data-driven healthcare analysis.

The dashboard explores how factors such as **age, smoking habits, air pollution exposure, tumor size, and treatment type** influence patient survival outcomes.

---

## Dataset Information

The dataset contains **2000 patient records** with the following attributes:

* Patient demographics (age, gender, country)
* Cancer stage and tumor size
* Smoking status
* Air pollution exposure
* Family medical history
* Treatment type
* Survival duration

---

## Tools Used

* Power BI
* Data Visualization
* Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

---

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. Executive Overview

Provides a summary of key metrics including:

* Total Patients
* Survival Rate
* Average Age
* Average Survival Months
* Gender distribution
* Cancer stage distribution

---

### 2. Patient Demographics & Risk Factors

Analyzes potential risk contributors including:

* Age distribution of patients
* Smoking status vs cancer stage
* Air pollution exposure
* Family history of lung cancer

---

### 3. Clinical & Treatment Insights

Examines medical factors affecting survival outcomes:

* Tumor size vs survival months
* Tumor category distribution
* Average survival months by treatment type

---

### 4. Global Patient Distribution

Displays geographic distribution of lung cancer patients across different countries.

---

## Key Insights

* Most patients belong to the **senior age group**.
* **Stage I has the highest number of diagnosed cases**.
* Current smokers show **higher proportions of Stage III and Stage IV cancer**.
* High air pollution exposure correlates with **higher patient counts**.
* Surgical treatments show the **highest average survival months**.

---

## Project Outcome

This dashboard demonstrates how healthcare data can be analyzed using business analytics tools to identify patterns, assess treatment effectiveness, and support informed decision-making.
